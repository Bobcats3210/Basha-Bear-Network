Who We Are

TELLING
BASHA'S
STORY

Established in August 2022, the Basha Bear Network is Basha High School's student-run broadcast and media program, housed within the TV and Film Production class. From breaking news to sports highlights, our student journalists record, edit, and produce original media covering school events and the broader community.

Every Friday at 10:30 AM we premiere our weekly newscast. Throughout the year, we also produce live sports broadcasts, podcasts, and social media content — gaining real-world skills in broadcast journalism, video production, audio engineering, and digital media.

How to Join

JOIN
THE
NETWORK

BBN doesn't take applications. To become part of the crew, complete MultiMedia & Film Year 1 as a freshman or sophomore — that's where you'll learn the foundations of broadcast journalism, video production, and storytelling. Once you've finished Year 1, you'll join the Basha Bear Network in Year 2 and start working on real broadcasts, sports streams, and competition pieces.

Talk to your counselor about adding MultiMedia & Film to next year's schedule.
