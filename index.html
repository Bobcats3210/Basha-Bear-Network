Who We Are

TELLING
BASHA'S
STORY

The Basha Bear Network is Basha High School's student-run broadcast and media program. From breaking news to sports highlights, we cover every story that matters to the Bear community.

Our team of student journalists, producers, editors, and on-air talent works together to create professional-quality content — gaining real-world skills in broadcast journalism, video production, audio engineering, and digital media.

🎙️

Broadcast

Live and recorded broadcasts covering school news and events.

🎬
